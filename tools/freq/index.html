Scope. Bands listed above are US (ITU Region 2) amateur allocations under FCC Part 97. Privileges within each band depend on license class — Technician, General, and Amateur Extra each see different sub-segments, especially on HF phone. The segment readout shows the general activity at the input frequency; it is not a substitute for the authoritative band plan. For full detail, see the ARRL US Amateur Radio Bands chart and FCC Part 97.301 / 97.305. The 2200 m and 630 m allocations require LF/MF notification to the UTC; 60 m is channelized with USB only and a 100 W ERP cap.
